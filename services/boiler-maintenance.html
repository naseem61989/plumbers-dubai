Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule boiler maintenance in Dubai's climate?
In Dubai, it is recommended to schedule boiler maintenance at least once a year due to the high temperatures and humidity, which can affect boiler efficiency. Regular checks help ensure safety and optimal operation.
What are common signs that my residential boiler needs maintenance?
Unusual noises, inconsistent heating, and increased energy bills can indicate that your boiler needs maintenance. Addressing these issues promptly can prevent more serious problems.
Can hard water in Dubai affect my boiler's performance?
Yes, the hard water prevalent in Dubai can lead to mineral buildup in your boiler, reducing efficiency and potentially causing damage. Regular descaling during maintenance is essential to mitigate this issue.
Is it necessary to inspect the boiler flue during maintenance?
Yes, inspecting the boiler flue is crucial as blockages can lead to hazardous carbon monoxide leaks. Ensuring the flue is clear and functioning properly is a vital part of boiler maintenance.
What should I do if my boiler is leaking?
If your boiler is leaking, it's important to turn it off immediately and contact a professional for assessment. Leaks can be due to pressure issues or faulty components, requiring expert attention to prevent further damage.
