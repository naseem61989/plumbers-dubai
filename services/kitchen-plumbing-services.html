Frequently Asked Questions

What types of kitchen plumbing issues are common in Dubai homes?
Common kitchen plumbing issues in Dubai homes include clogged drains, leaky faucets, and malfunctioning garbage disposals. Due to the hard water in the region, scale buildup in pipes and fixtures is also a frequent problem.
How can I prevent scale buildup in my kitchen plumbing?
To prevent scale buildup, consider installing a water softener to treat hard water before it enters your plumbing system. Regularly cleaning faucets and fixtures can also help reduce mineral deposits and maintain efficient water flow.
What should I do if I suspect a leak under my kitchen sink?
If you suspect a leak, first check for visible signs of water accumulation and moisture. Turn off the water supply to the sink and contact a professional plumber to assess and repair the leak to prevent further damage.
How often should I schedule professional maintenance for my kitchen plumbing?
It's advisable to schedule a professional plumbing inspection and maintenance at least once a year. Regular maintenance helps identify potential issues early, ensuring your kitchen plumbing operates efficiently and prolongs the lifespan of your fixtures and appliances.
Why is my kitchen drain frequently clogging, and how can I prevent it?
Frequent clogs can result from grease buildup, food particles, or foreign objects obstructing the pipe. To prevent this, avoid pouring grease down the drain, use a strainer to catch food scraps, and periodically flush the drain with hot water or a baking soda-vinegar solution.
