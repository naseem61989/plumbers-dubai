Frequently Asked Questions

What are the signs that my angle valve needs replacement in Dubai?
Common signs include water leaks around the valve, difficulty in turning the valve handle, or poor water flow. If you notice these issues, it's advisable to get a professional assessment.
How often should angle valves be replaced in Dubai?
Angle valves typically last 8-10 years, but this can vary based on water quality and usage. Regular inspections can help determine the optimal time for replacement.
Are there specific angle valve brands recommended for Dubai homes?
Yes, brands that offer corrosion-resistant materials are recommended due to the high mineral content in Dubai's water. Consult with a local plumbing expert for the best options.
What are the benefits of replacing old angle valves?
Replacing old angle valves ensures reliable water flow, reduces the risk of leaks, and helps maintain water pressure. New valves can also prevent potential water damage to your property.
Can I replace an angle valve myself, or should I hire a professional in Dubai?
While some homeowners attempt DIY replacements, hiring a professional ensures the job is done correctly and safely. Professionals also have the tools and expertise to handle any unforeseen issues.
