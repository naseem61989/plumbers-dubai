Common signs include inconsistent water temperatures, unusual noises from the tank, water discoloration, or leaks around the unit. If you experience any of these, it's advisable to contact a professional for a thorough inspection.

Yes, we specialize in repairing both electric and gas water heaters. Our technicians are trained to handle a variety of models and brands, ensuring efficient and reliable repair services tailored to your specific unit.
