Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if my water pump is making unusual noises?
Unusual noises from a water pump can indicate air in the system, worn bearings, or a failing motor. First, try bleeding the system to remove any trapped air. If the noise persists, it's advisable to contact a professional plumber in Dubai to inspect and repair the pump.
How often should I schedule a plumbing inspection for my Dubai property?
It's recommended to have a professional plumbing inspection at least once a year. Regular inspections can help identify potential issues early, such as leaks or blockages, ensuring the longevity and efficiency of your plumbing systems.
What are the signs that my plumbing system might have a blockage?
Signs of a blockage include slow draining sinks, gurgling sounds from drains, and unpleasant odors. If you notice any of these symptoms, it’s important to address them promptly to prevent further damage to your plumbing system.
Why is my water bill suddenly higher than usual?
A sudden increase in your water bill could be due to hidden leaks in your plumbing system. Check for visible leaks or running toilets, and if none are found, consult a Dubai-based plumber to conduct a thorough leak detection service.
Is it necessary to replace old plumbing pipes in Dubai?
Old pipes, especially those made from galvanized steel or lead, should be replaced to prevent leaks and health hazards. A professional plumber can assess the condition of your pipes and recommend the best course of action for replacement, ensuring your plumbing system is up to Dubai's current standards.
