Frequently Asked Questions

How can I identify if my Ariston water heater needs repairs?
If your Ariston water heater is producing inconsistent temperatures, making unusual noises, or showing leaks around the unit, these are signs it may require repairs. Regular maintenance can prevent these issues from escalating.
What should I do if my Ariston water heater is leaking?
Immediately turn off the water supply and power to your heater to prevent further damage. Contact a professional service in Dubai to diagnose and repair the leak safely and effectively.
Why is my Ariston water heater producing rusty water?
Rusty water often indicates corrosion inside the tank, which may require replacing the anode rod or the tank itself. It's crucial to address this promptly to avoid further damage.
How often should I service my Ariston water heater in Dubai?
It's recommended to service your Ariston water heater at least once a year. Regular maintenance helps ensure efficient operation and can prolong the lifespan of your unit, especially in Dubai's climate.
Can I perform repairs on my Ariston water heater myself?
While some minor issues like thermostat adjustments can be handled by homeowners, most repairs, especially involving electrical components or plumbing, should be conducted by certified professionals to ensure safety and compliance with local regulations.
